WHAT IT SHOWS
A summary of AI's overnight activity across 6 connected systems. Shows exactly 2 items that need Vikram's decision today — a pipeline risk and an expiring offer. Everything else (37 items) was handled autonomously.
WHY IT'S HERE FIRST
A leader's first question every morning is: "What needs me today?" This section answers that in 10 seconds — with context already included. No hunting through emails, Slack, or dashboards.
DECISION ITEMS
Item 1 (Red dot · Critical) — Platform pipeline slipped 8%. AI has already computed the fix: reassign one engineer from Growth. Vikram just needs to approve or see the plan.

Item 2 (Amber dot · Urgent) — Offer expires at 5pm. Competing offer exists. AI recommends +6% band. One tap to approve or review the details.
DESIGN CHOICES
Lavender tinted card signals AI content, not a regular notification.

Color-coded dots (red = critical, amber = time-sensitive) give urgency at a glance without reading.

"AI handled 37 overnight" accordion builds trust — Vikram can verify what AI did before approving the remaining 2.
LEADER BENEFIT
Replaces 20–30 minutes of morning triage. Instead of opening 5 systems, Vikram sees exactly what needs his attention — with the AI's recommended action already surfaced. The accordion gives full auditability if he wants to verify what AI handled independently.

Status pills (colored dot + label) give semantic meaning beyond just a number.

WHAT IT SHOWS
3 items across different signal types:

POLICY (Action needed) — Hybrid work policy goes live Monday. 2 remote pods need a desk plan from Vikram.
WIN (FYI) — Platform cut p95 latency 40%. Good for today's review shout-out.
PEOPLE (FYI) — Aanya returns from 4-month leave. Know before the day's standup.
ACTION NEEDED vs. FYI CHIPS
The amber "Action needed" chip flags that the POLICY item requires Vikram to respond before Monday. The gray "FYI" chip signals informational items — read them, but no follow-up required.
WHY LEADERS NEED THIS
Policy — A leader who misses a rollout and hasn't planned for remote pods gets caught in a meeting on Monday.

Wins — Recognising a team win in today's review builds morale. The dashboard prompts this.

People — Knowing Aanya is back lets Vikram re-assign work or say hello first thing.
DESIGN CHOICE — SIGNAL TRIAGE
Before this chip system, all 3 items looked identical — leaders treated them all as informational and missed the policy action. The chips remove that ambiguity at first glance.
